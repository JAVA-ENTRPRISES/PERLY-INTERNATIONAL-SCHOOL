Campus Facilities

Our campus provides modern infrastructure and resources that support academic excellence, creativity, and student well-being.

World-Class Learning Environment

Mother Teresa E-Techno School offers modern facilities designed to support both academic and extracurricular development. Our campus provides a safe, engaging, and inspiring environment where students can explore their talents and grow into confident individuals.

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms equipped with smart boards, projectors, and digital learning tools to make lessons more interactive and engaging.

Modern Library

A well-organized library with a wide collection of academic, reference, and story books that encourage reading habits and knowledge exploration.

Computer Laboratory

Modern computers with internet connectivity where students learn digital literacy, programming basics, and technology skills.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs where students learn scientific concepts through experiments and practical activities.

Sports Facilities

Indoor and outdoor sports facilities that promote fitness, teamwork, discipline, and healthy competition among students.

Auditorium

A spacious auditorium where cultural events, seminars, performances, and school programs are conducted.

Safe & Secure Campus

Student safety is our top priority. Our campus is monitored by CCTV cameras, supervised by trained staff, and follows strict safety protocols to provide a secure learning environment.
